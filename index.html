About me


As a teenager at school, I used to be the one everybody referred to for any technical problem they were facing. Since then, identifying and solving these sorts of issues has become a part of my routine.

After finishing my bachelor's in IT Engineering back in Iran, I enthusiastically continued coding, but due to some circumstances, I could not pursue my dream of becoming a full-time programmer earlier. Fortunately, I have had the opportunity to retrain as a Web Developer to be able to finally turn my passion into my profession.

I have a few years of work experience in administration, management, and customer service that has helped me become an adaptable problem solver who enjoys teamwork as much as individual projects and loves to share ideas and prioritize communication to achieve the goals within the deadlines.

I embrace new challenges and any projects that would require me to think out of the box. With the goal of improving myself and my skills every day, I can consider myself a lifelong learner.

ChitChat

ChitChat is a chat app for mobile devices developed using React Native + Gifted Chat and Firebase Firestore. The app will provide users with a chat interface and options to share images and their location. Users are able to enter their name and select a color for their chat background. Users can send text messages, images (from the gallery and taken with the camera) and own location. Messages will be stored and displayed when the user is off-line.

  • React Native
  • Expo
  • Firestore/Firebase
  • Gifted Chat
