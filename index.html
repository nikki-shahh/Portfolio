About me

My name is Hossnieh (AKA Nikki). I’m a Los Angeles based Full Stack Developer. As a teenager in school, I was always the kid other students approached about their phones or computers issues. Since then, it’s become such a normal occurrence that I’m always the one resolving any technical issues. After graduating from IT Engineering in Iran, I found myself loving coding and programming. Unfortunately, I could not pursue this dream earlier due to enforced restrictions. But I have recently had the opportunity to retrain as a Web Developer to turn my passion into my profession. I also have a background in administration, management, and customer service. I am skilled at identifying problems and gathering requirements and a well-trained teammate who loves to share ideas and prioritize communication to achieve the goals within the deadlines. I like the challenges and working on projects which make me come out of my comfort zone. I consider myself a lifelong learner, and I'm constantly pursuing opportunities to stay current.

ChitChat

ChitChat is a chat app for mobile devices developed using React Native + Gifted Chat and Firebase Firestore. The app will provide users with a chat interface and options to share images and their location. Users are able to enter their name and select a color for their chat background. Users can send text messages, images (from the gallery and taken with the camera) and own location. Messages will be stored and displayed when the user is off-line.
